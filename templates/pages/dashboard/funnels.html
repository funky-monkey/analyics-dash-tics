What is a funnel?

A funnel tracks how visitors move through a sequence of steps — for example: homepage → pricing → signup. At each step you see how many visitors continued and how many dropped off. This tells you exactly where in your flow you're losing people.

Steps can be pages or events. Use a URL for a pageview step, or an event name (e.g. button_click) for a custom event step.
Works on existing data. Creating a funnel now immediately shows results from all your historical events — no need to wait for new traffic.
Steps must be in order. A visitor is counted in step 2 only if they completed step 1 first — in chronological order.
